**Blinkit Sales Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)**

Project Overview
This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyze Blinkit’s sales performance across outlets, item categories, outlet size, and location tiers.  
The goal is to derive business insights related to sales trends, product performance, and outlet efficiency.

Tools & Technologies
Power BI Desktop
Data Modeling
DAX Measures
Data Visualization & Dashboarding

Dataset Information
The dataset contains sales-related information including:
- Item details (type, fat content, visibility)
- Outlet details (type, size, location tier)
- Sales, ratings, and quantity metrics

Dataset used only for learning and portfolio purposes.*

Key KPIs
- **Total Sales:** $1.20M  
- **Average Sales:** $141  
- **Number of Items:** 8,523  
- **Average Rating:** 3.9  

Dashboard Insights
Sales Trend by Outlet Establishment
- Sales show steady growth with a peak around **2018**
- Slight decline observed post-2019
- 
Item Type Performance
- Top contributing categories:
  - Fruits & Vegetables
  - Snack Foods
  - Household items
- Seafood contributes the least to total sales

Fat Content Analysis
Regular fat items contribute more sales than low-fat items
Tier 3 outlets lead in fat-content-based sales

Outlet Size Contribution
Medium-sized outlets generate the highest sales
High-size outlets contribute the least

Outlet Location Insights
Tier 3 locations outperform Tier 1 & Tier 2 in total sales

Outlet Type Comparison
| Outlet Type | Total Sales | No. of Items | Avg Sales | Avg Rating |
 Supermarket Type1 | Highest | 5,577 | $141 | 3.9 |
 Grocery Store | Moderate | 1,083 | $140 | 3.9 |
 Supermarket Type2 & 3 | Lower | ~900 | $140+ | 3.9 |

 Business Takeaways
- Focus expansion on **Tier 3 locations**
- Medium-sized outlets deliver the **best ROI**
- Inventory optimization needed for low-performing categories
